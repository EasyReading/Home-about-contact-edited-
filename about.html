Easy Reading Overviews

The Easy reading is a web application which will be developed as an assistive tool for knowledge seekers, who wants to increase his /her knowledge by reading book. The goals of our project are to provide various types of books in online for reading and selling to the all types of people.

Users are the very important part of a system. Before developing a system, first we need to find out the requirements of the different group of users and how they interact with the system. To show the interaction between user and system MVC architectural pattern will be helpful. The MVC architectural pattern describe the overall shape of the system and also show the interaction between the system and user.

The Easy reading is a web application will helpful for knowledge seekers. They can learn and earn difference types of knowledge using our system easily. Because all the book keep in our system according to category.
